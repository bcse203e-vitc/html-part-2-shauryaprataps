Paris, France

Known as the city of lights, Paris offers timeless beauty and romance with iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum.

Travel Tips:

  • Book tickets to popular attractions in advance.
  • Use the Paris Metro for affordable transportation.
  • Don't miss a picnic at Champ de Mars.

Sydney, Australia

Explore Sydney's iconic beaches, landmarks like the Opera House, and the warm Australian hospitality.

Travel Tips:

  • Climb the Sydney Harbour Bridge for breathtaking views.
  • Enjoy Bondi Beach and the coastal walk to Coogee.
  • Explore the Royal Botanic Garden.
