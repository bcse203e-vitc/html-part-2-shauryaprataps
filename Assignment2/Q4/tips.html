Packing Tips

  • Roll your clothes to save space and avoid wrinkles.
  • Carry a reusable water bottle to stay hydrated.
  • Use packing cubes to keep your luggage organized.
  • Always pack a portable charger and universal adapter.

Money-Saving Tips

  • Book flights and accommodations in advance for the best deals.
  • Use public transportation instead of taxis.
  • Look for free walking tours to explore the city.
  • Try local street food for authentic and budget-friendly meals.

Health & Safety Tips

  • Keep a first aid kit handy for emergencies.
  • Check travel advisories and follow local guidelines.
  • Stay alert and avoid displaying expensive items in public.
  • Carry copies of your passport and travel documents.
