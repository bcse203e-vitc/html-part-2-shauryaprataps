Spaghetti

  • Home

Ingredients

  • 300g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. In a frying pan, heat olive oil and saut√© the pancetta until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Parmesan, and Pecorino cheese.
  4. Drain the pasta, reserving a little pasta water, then add the hot pasta to the pancetta.
  5. Remove the pan from heat and quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture. Add reserved pasta water to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, then serve with extra Parmesan on top.
